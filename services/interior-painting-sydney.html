Our Process

How We Deliver Perfect Results

Every KPSS Painting interior project follows our proven 5-step process to ensure consistent, premium results.

1. Free Consultation & Quote

We visit your property, assess every surface, discuss your colour preferences, and provide a detailed written quote — completely free, no obligation.

2. Surface Preparation

Proper preparation is 80% of a great paint job. We fill holes, sand rough patches, scrape flaking paint, clean surfaces, and apply primer where needed.

3. Protection & Masking

All furniture, floors, fixtures, and fittings are covered with drop sheets and masked with quality painter's tape to protect your property.

4. Expert Application

Two coats minimum of premium paint — brush cutting-in at edges and corners, followed by even roller application for a flawless finish. We use Dulux and Taubmans paints exclusively.

5. Inspection & Cleanup

Final walkthrough with you to ensure every detail meets your expectations. Complete cleanup — we leave your space spotless and ready to enjoy.

Common Questions

Interior Painting FAQs

Interior painting in Sydney typically costs $20–$60 per square metre, depending on room size, ceiling height, surface condition, and paint quality. A standard 3-bedroom home interior ranges from $3,000–$8,000. KPSS Painting provides free, detailed quotes tailored to your project. Call +61 410 818 849.
A single room typically takes 1–2 days. A full 3-bedroom home interior takes 3–5 days including preparation, priming, two coats, and cleanup. Larger homes or properties requiring extensive prep work may take longer. We provide accurate timelines with every quote.
We exclusively use premium Australian paint brands: Dulux Wash & Wear for high-traffic areas, Dulux Ceiling White for ceilings, and Taubmans for specialty finishes. All paints are low-VOC for better indoor air quality and long-lasting durability.
We recommend moving small items and valuables. For larger furniture, we can move it to the centre of the room and cover it with protective drop sheets. KPSS Painting fully protects all floors, fixtures, and fittings during the painting process.
