Sydney Climate

Painted to Withstand Sydney's Weather

Sydney's climate is demanding on exterior surfaces — intense UV radiation, coastal salt spray, summer humidity, and heavy winter rains. KPSS Painting uses only premium paints specifically formulated for Australian conditions.

UV Protection

Sydney receives some of the highest UV levels globally. We use paints with advanced UV stabilisers that resist fading and chalking, keeping your colours vibrant for 10+ years.

Moisture Resistance

Breathable, water-resistant coatings that prevent moisture penetration while allowing walls to "breathe" — critical for preventing trapped moisture, mould, and paint bubbling.

Mould & Mildew Prevention

Anti-fungal additives in our exterior paint systems inhibit mould and mildew growth, especially important for Sydney's south-facing and shaded walls.

Crack Bridging

Elastomeric exterior paints flex with temperature changes, bridging hairline cracks and preventing new cracks from forming. Essential for rendered surfaces in Sydney's variable climate.

Exterior Painting FAQs

Exterior painting in Sydney typically costs $30–$80 per square metre. A full single-storey house exterior ranges from $5,000–$15,000 depending on size, height, surface type, and preparation needed. Multi-storey homes cost more due to scaffolding requirements. KPSS Painting provides free on-site quotes.
For Sydney's UV-intense, humid climate, premium acrylic paints with UV stabilisers and mould inhibitors are ideal. We recommend Dulux Weathershield and Taubmans Endure for maximum durability — typically lasting 10–15 years when professionally applied with proper preparation.
Most exterior paint jobs last 7–12 years in Sydney depending on surface, paint quality, and sun exposure. North-facing walls fade faster. Signs you need repainting include chalking, peeling, fading, or visible cracks. Regular pressure washing can extend the life of your paint.
Yes, Sydney's mild winters (10–18°C) are generally suitable for exterior painting. We monitor temperature and humidity carefully — professional exterior paints require at least 10°C and no rain for 24 hours. We schedule around weather forecasts to ensure optimal drying and adhesion.
