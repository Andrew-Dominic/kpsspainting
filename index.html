Everything you need to know about hiring a professional painter in Sydney.

The cost of hiring a painter in Sydney typically ranges from $20–$60 per square metre for interior work and $30–$80 per square metre for exterior work, depending on the surface condition, height access, paint quality, and project scope. KPSS Painting provides free, no-obligation quotes tailored to your specific project. Call us at +61 410 818 849 for a personalised estimate.
Yes, KPSS Painting is fully licensed (NSW Licence No. 469375C) and insured. We are also ABN registered (71 674 955 367), ensuring full accountability and professionalism on every project. Our licence is verified through NSW Fair Trading.
KPSS Painting serves all of Greater Sydney including Parramatta, North Sydney, Western Sydney, Blacktown, Penrith, Liverpool, Campbelltown, Bankstown, Bondi, Manly, Chatswood, Hornsby, Sutherland, Cronulla, and surrounding suburbs across NSW. We service both residential homes and commercial properties.
A standard 3-bedroom house interior typically takes 3–5 days to paint professionally, while a full exterior can take 4–7 days depending on the condition and weather. KPSS Painting provides accurate timelines with every quote and guarantees on-time project completion.
Yes. In New South Wales, any painting work valued at over $5,000 (including labour and materials) legally requires a licensed contractor. Hiring an unlicensed painter means you have no protection under the Home Building Act. KPSS Painting holds NSW Licence No. 469375C, verified by NSW Fair Trading.
For Sydney's humid subtropical climate, exterior surfaces benefit from premium acrylic paints with UV resistance and mould-inhibiting properties — brands like Dulux Weathershield and Taubmans Endure perform exceptionally well. For interiors, low-VOC washable paints provide durability and better air quality. KPSS Painting uses only premium Australian paints suited to local conditions.
We offer a comprehensive range of professional painting services: interior painting (walls, ceilings, trims, doors), exterior painting (weatherboard, brick, render, cladding), wall finishing (filling, sanding, priming, skim coating), commercial painting (offices, retail, warehouses), and precision touch-up repairs for both residential and commercial properties across Sydney.
Before your painter arrives: 1) Move furniture away from walls or to the centre of rooms. 2) Remove wall hangings, curtain rods, and switch plates. 3) Clear driveways and pathways for exterior work. 4) Secure pets in a safe area. 5) Point out any specific concerns like cracks, water damage, or mould. KPSS Painting handles all masking, drop sheeting, and surface preparation as part of our professional service.
