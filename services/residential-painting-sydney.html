Our Approach

The KPSS Residential Experience

Colour Consultation

Not sure about colours? We provide expert advice on colour selection based on your room's natural light, architecture, and personal style. We can arrange sample pots so you can see colours in your actual space before committing.

Family-Friendly Process

We understand that your home is your sanctuary. Our team works cleanly, quietly, and respectfully. We contain dust, use low-odour paints, and clean up thoroughly every day. Your family can live comfortably while we work.

Furniture Protection

Full drop-sheeting of all floors and furniture. Careful masking of fixtures, fittings, and finishes. Boot covers throughout your home. We leave your space cleaner than we found it.

Workmanship Guarantee

Every residential project comes with our workmanship guarantee. If any area doesn't meet the agreed standard, we return and fix it free of charge. We're not done until you're 100% satisfied.
